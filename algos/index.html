Design Structures Review

The other day, I picked up my old Algorithm Design textbook from college, and realized I remembered very little of it. I thought a bit about what exactly I wanted to remember, and decided to write up some very simple summaries of key structures to provide a jumping off point to dive back into the topic.

My goal with each is for it to be super quick (less than a few hundred words each), just providing enough detail to act as a client of the structure and understand key trade-offs.

Structures

  • Graph Searching - BFS and DFS
  • Sorting - Quick Sort and Merge Sort
  • Minimum Spanning Trees - Kruskal's and Prim's Algorithms
  • Shortest Path between Nodes - Dijkstra's Algorithm
  • Dynamic Programming - Knapsack Problem
  • Network Flow - Bipartite Matching
